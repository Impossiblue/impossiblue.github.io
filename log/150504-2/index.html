Rain on a window pane – JavaScript simulation

5 March, 2017 – Art. 27 – First published on Ka of Isis 150504

Fig. The beauty of the effect is enhanced by the striking photograph by Indonesian photographer and artist Thongart. If you, the visitor, see no raindrops, your browser probably does not support the canvas element. Your loss.

If simulated realism is a contradiction in terms, then Marek Brodziak’s jaw-dropping RainyDay rain-on-a-windowpane JavaScript animation surely bridges the gap. The simulation uses the canvas element to good effect as small raindrops combine to form larger water drops which eventually float gracefully down the window’s glass. Yes, the glass is there. Look!
